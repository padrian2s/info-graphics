Quick decision matrix pick your shape

  • Ambiguity high? colleague-shaped.
  • Spec crisp? tool-shaped.
  • Risk costly? colleague-shaped (until tests/spec mature).
  • Parallel tasks? tool-shaped (spin up multiple runs).
  • Skill distribution wide? mixed mode (colleague for juniors, tool for seniors).
