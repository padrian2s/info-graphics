The 8 building blocks

Engineering principles in plain language

1) Dropbox

One frictionless capture point. One action. One habit. (Slack channel.)

2) Sorter

AI classifier/router assigns the bucket so you don‚Äôt decide at capture time.

3) Form

A schema/data contract: consistent fields enable reliable automation and summaries.

4) Filing cabinet

Writable by automation, readable by humans, filterable by views. (Notion DBs.)

5) Receipt

Audit trail/ledger: what came in, what happened, why, and confidence score.

6) Bouncer

Confidence filter: low certainty ‚Üí hold + ask for clarification, not bad filing.

7) Tap on the shoulder

Proactive surfacing: daily digest + weekly review delivered where you already are.

8) Fix button

One-step correction. If repair is hard, adoption dies.
