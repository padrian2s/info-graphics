The dimensions you said you’ll examine

These are the “inputs” that determine whether a country can turn AI coding agents into real software delivery throughput.

Digital infrastructure

Connectivity, cloud access, device availability, reliable power, modern tooling baseline.

Developer education

CS fundamentals, engineering rigor, ability to validate outputs, test-driven habits.

English + “specification language”

Ability to express precise requirements, constraints, acceptance criteria, and edge cases.

Openness to automation

Low stigma around tooling changes, high tolerance for iteration, “ship–measure–fix” mindset.

Entrepreneurial ecosystem

Startups + venture + fast procurement + talent density + strong OSS participation.

AI readiness (governance + data)

Regulation clarity, data availability, security posture, and enterprise willingness to deploy.

Interpretation Countries “win” by combining high-quality specs + fast validation loops + deployment freedom. The rest is acceleration.
