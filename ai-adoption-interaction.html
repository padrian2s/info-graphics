The ‚Äúmanager‚Äù toolkit

Two winning work patterns

Centaurs split responsibilities cleanly; cyborgs integrate continuously. The 201 skill is choosing the right mode per task.

Centaur mode

Clear boundaries, clear accountability

Best for high-stakes work. The human owns framing, decisions, and verification checkpoints. The AI generates options, drafts, and summaries within controlled scope.

When centaur wins

Legal language, policy documents, compliance-sensitive writing, medical or safety-critical communications, executive decisions, and anywhere ‚Äúwho is responsible?‚Äù must be unambiguous.

StrengthStrong human judgment + controlled delegation
Risk controlExplicit checkpoints and verification gates
Failure modeOver-delegation, vague constraints, skipped review

Cyborg mode

Continuous collaboration, fluid boundaries

Best for creative and iterative work. The human and AI co-create: brainstorm, draft, critique, reframe, and refine in many small turns.

StrengthFast iteration loops and exploration

Failure modeDrift, hidden errors, vague ‚Äúgood enough‚Äù
